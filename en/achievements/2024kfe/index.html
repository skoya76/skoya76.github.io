💵 Grant: Overseas Training Support for Graduate Students

Jul 18, 2024 · 1 min read

I studied at the Technical University of Munich as part of the TUMexchange Program, from September 2024 to February 2025. This exchange was supported by the Graduate Student Overseas Training Grant, funded by the KAMINO FOUNDATION FOR EDUCATION, which provided 300,000 yen. This scholarship is designed for graduate students with strong academic performance and exemplary character, particularly those with ties to the Aichi Higashi-Mikawa region.
